Interests

Food Technology

I'm deeply interested in food technology; especially in quality control, post-harvest loss prevention, sustainability, and edible films. These interests stem from a desire to solve real-world problems in food systems, reduce waste, and improve food packaging through innovative and sustainable solutions.

During my time as a Food Science and Technology student, I gained hands-on experience during my industrial training, working on the quality testing of soft drinks, preforms, and closures. My final-year project focused on creating edible film from agricultural waste, blending innovation with environmental consciousness. That project was a defining moment for me and sparked a deeper commitment to sustainability in food science.
